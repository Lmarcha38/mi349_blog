About

My name is Luke Marchand and I am an undergraduate student at Michigan State University. My major is Games and Interactive Media. Of course, I love playing video games!

My top 5 favorite games in order are:

  1. Titanfall 2
  2. Xenoblade Chronicles 2
  3. Pokemon Heartgold
  4. Pokemon Legends Arceus
  5. Persona 3 Reload

I don't tend to play very many video games these days because I now spend most of my time creating video games! I've spent many years of my life now creating numerous video game projects. However, I've only released 2 of these projects to the public as games for people to play, and one of them is no longer available. Since I've been doing this for so long, I believe more and more of my projects will be released since I am past the initial learning stage. My project that I am currently working on, called SauceLore, is getting close to being ready for release. SauceLore will be available to download on Steam, and there is already a SauceLore Steam page where you can wishlist the game and download a demo I made.
